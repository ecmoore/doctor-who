The Eleventh Doctor

Born, “still cooking”, into a crashing TARDIS, the Eleventh Doctor hurtles into the life of Amy Pond, and the mystery of the crack in her wall. Pursuing the time-erasing cracks, the Doctor and Amy battle new paradigm Daleks in World War Two, Weeping Angels by the thousand and the depression in Vincent van Gogh’s mind. After learning the cracks are caused by the TARDIS exploding, the Doctor sacrifices himself to reboot the universe, only to be remembered back into existence by Amy. After calling his companions to Lake Silenco, Utah, a future version of the Doctor was seen to die at the hands of an impossible astronaut. When it transpired that the astronaut was River Song, controlled by the Silence, the Doctor allowed the fixed moment in time to happen by substituting himself with shape-shifting ship Teselecta.
